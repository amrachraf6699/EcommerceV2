# Phase 6: Cart and Checkout

## Goal
Complete the buying flow from cart review to order placement.

## Scope
- Build cart page.
- Build checkout page or checkout flow.
- Add pricing summary helpers for:
  - subtotal
  - shipping
  - VAT
  - grand total
- Connect customer address entry and selection.
- Keep forms sharp, modern, and easy to scan on mobile.

## Deliverables
- Cart screen with quantity updates and removal actions.
- Checkout summary and form flow tied to current pricing rules.
- Shipping and VAT displayed clearly using current settings.

## Test Checklist
- Cart quantity and removal actions work.
- Totals calculate correctly with shipping and VAT.
- Checkout form works in RTL on mobile and desktop.
- Order placement path reaches the expected result.

## Acceptance
- Cart and checkout are usable end-to-end.
- Totals are trustworthy and clearly explained.
- Mobile checkout does not feel cramped or fragile.
