# Phase 5: Product Details

## Goal
Build the product details experience so customers can inspect product media, choose variants, and add items to cart confidently.

## Scope
- Build the product details page.
- Add product gallery interactions.
- Add variant selection behavior.
- Show stock, price, and product media clearly.
- Add sticky purchase actions on mobile if needed.
- Show shipping and VAT cues in the product summary area.

## Deliverables
- Product page using real product, variant, and image data.
- Gallery and variant interactions enhanced with lightweight JS.
- Purchase section optimized for mobile readability.

## Test Checklist
- Variant selection updates the visible state correctly.
- Product gallery works across screen sizes.
- Product images and metadata render correctly.
- Add-to-cart path is clear and stable.

## Acceptance
- PDP feels complete and high quality.
- Customers can understand pricing, media, and variants without friction.
- The page is ready to feed cart and checkout flows.
